About

My name is Jiefeng Sun, a Ph.D. student at Colorado State University in Mechanical Engineering since Aug, 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Zhao. My research focuses on the design, modeling, and control of soft robots and artificial muscles (high-energy-density small actuators). I earned my B.S. and M.S. in Mechanical Engineering respectively from Lanzhou University of Technology Lanzhou in 2014 and Dalian University of Technology (DLUT) in 2017.

I am looking for a postdoc position in the robotics field starting from June of 2022. Please find my CV here. Thanks!
