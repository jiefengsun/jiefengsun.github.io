About

My name is Jiefeng Sun, a PhD student at Colorado State University in Mechanical Engineering since Aug, 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Zhao. My research focuses on design, modeling and control of soft robotics and high-energy-density artificial muscle. I was born in Xuchang, Henan, China and earned my B.S. and M.S. in Mechanical Engineering respecitively from Lanzhou University of Technology Lanzhou in 2014 and Dalian University of Technology (DLUT) in 2017.

Artificial Muscle; Robotic Modeling; Soft Robotics;
